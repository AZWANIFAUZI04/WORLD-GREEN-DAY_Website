DRIVING DIRECTIONS

Follow these steps to reach the event:

  • Take 14 minutes to Dewan Aspirasi.
  • Go straight and turn right into UiTM Dungun.
  • Turn left near Pos A and go straight.
  • The event location is on your left-hand side.

ADDITIONAL INFORMATION

The registration desk will be set up at the main entrance on the ground floor.
